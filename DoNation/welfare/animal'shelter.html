  • GO NIRVANA FOUNDATION

    GO NIRVANA FOUNDATION
    Go Nirvana-The Subhadra Devi Foundation
    is a not-for-profit, registered organization
    in India committed to the welfare of voiceless
    species (animals). In our country animals
    including dogs, cows, cats, and donkeys
    live on the streets and they meet very
    unpleasant deaths. Our mission is to provide
    proper cremation to such strays & abandoned
    animals, it’s a mission to inspire our human
    community to deliver the same treatment which
    we deliver to our own community. Our vision is
    to contribute to creating a world where humans
    treat animals with respect, empathy, and understanding.

  • ISKON GAUSHALA

    ISKON GAUSHALA
    There are so many facilities afforded by cow protection, but people have forgotten these arts. The importance of protecting cows is therefore stressed by Krishna in Bhagavad-gita (krsi-go-raksya-vanijyam vaisya-karma svabhavajam [Bg. 18.44]). Even now in the Indian villages surrounding Vrndavana, the villagers live happily simply by giving protection to the cow. They keep cow dung very carefully and dry it to use as fuel. They keep a sufficient stock of grains, and because of giving protection to the cows, they have sufficient milk and milk products to solve all economic problems.
